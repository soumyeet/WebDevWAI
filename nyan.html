Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a job?
Click the "View More" button on any company card to see the full job description and application process. You'll need to upload your resume and fill out a short form.
What happens after I submit my application?
Our AI system will review your application and match it with the job requirements. You'll receive an email within 48 hours about the next steps in the process.
How can I prepare for the interview?
Once your application is accepted, you'll get access to our AI interview preparation tool. It includes common questions, company-specific information, and practice sessions.
